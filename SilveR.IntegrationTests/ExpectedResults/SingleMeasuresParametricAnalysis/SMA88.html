Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 1-way ANOVA approach, with Treat24 as the treatment factor. This was followed by Planned Comparisons of the predicted means to compare the levels of the Treat24 factor, Snedecor and Cochran (1989).

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
