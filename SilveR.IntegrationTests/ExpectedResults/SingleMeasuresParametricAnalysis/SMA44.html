Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 3-way ANOVA approach, with Treat1, Treat2 and Treat3 as the treatment factors. This was followed by Planned Comparisons of the predicted means to compare the levels of the Treat1 * Treat2 interaction, Snedecor and Cochran (1989). The response was log10 transformed prior to analysis to stabilise the variance.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
