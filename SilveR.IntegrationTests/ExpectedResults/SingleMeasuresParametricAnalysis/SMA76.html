Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 2-way ANOVA approach, with CVTreat1 and CVTreat2 as the treatment factors. This was followed by Planned Comparisons of the predicted means to compare the levels of the CVTreat1 factor, Snedecor and Cochran (1989).

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
