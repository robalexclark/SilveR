Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 2-way ANOVA approach, with Treat1 and Treat2 as the treatment factors and Blo ck1 and Blo ck2 as the blocking factors. This was followed by Planned Comparisons of the predicted means to compare the levels of the Treat2 factor, Snedecor and Cochran (1989). The response was square root transformed prior to analysis to stabilise the variance.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
