Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 2-way ANOVA approach, with IFTreat1 and IFTreat2 as the treatment factors. This was followed by Planned Comparisons of the predicted means to compare the levels of the IFTreat1 * IFTreat2 interaction, Snedecor and Cochran (1989).

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
