Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 3-way ANOVA approach, with Treat1, Treat2 and Treat3 as the treatment factors. This was followed by comparisons of the predicted means of the Treat1 factor back to the control group mean using Benjamini-Hochberg's procedure, Benjamini and Hochberg (1995). This was followed by all pairwise comparisons between the predicted means of the Treat1 factor using Benjamini-Hochberg's procedure, Benjamini and Hochberg (1995).

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
