Warning

Unfortunately there is complete separation in the predictors. Separation occurs in logistic regression when the factors perfectly predict the outcome and may produce infinite estimates for some model coefficients, see Mansournia et al. (2018). Care should be taken when reviewing this analysis as the results in the analysis of deviance table, odds ratios, confidence intervals and model predictions may not be reliable. You could try simplifying the statistical model to avoid this issue.
